Company Milestones

Shaivika IT Technologies was established in Kadapa with a vision to bring world-class digital solutions to local businesses.

2026

AI Division Launch

Introduced AI automation services — WhatsApp bots, Telegram agents, and intelligent dashboard systems for businesses.
