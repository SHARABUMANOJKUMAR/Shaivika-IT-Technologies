The Challenge

Manspharshcare needed a complete digital transformation — moving from manual appointment booking via phone calls to a fully automated, patient-friendly online portal that reduced staff workload and improved patient experience.

Our Solution

We built a full-stack healthcare platform with an intuitive patient portal, doctor scheduling system, automated WhatsApp reminders, telemedicine integration, and a powerful admin dashboard for the clinical team.

Results Delivered

  • 3x increase in online appointment bookings within 30 days
  • 95% faster page load times vs previous website
  • Automated WhatsApp reminders reduced no-shows by 60%
  • Staff saved 40% of their time on administrative tasks
  • Fully responsive across all devices and screen sizes
